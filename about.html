Our Story

Sweet Cake Studio was founded in 2026 in Pretoria, South Africa. The business is a small home-based bakery, specialising in custom cakes for birthdays, weddings and special events.

Due to an increase of sales, the business has grown into a recognised local brand known for its high-end creative designs and high-quality ingredients.

Mission Statement

To create delicious, designed cakes to make every of your events memorable. That offers you a smooth and reliable services with high-end cakes that are made with love, passion and drive.

Vision Statement

To become a leading custom cake provider known for their creativity, quality and exceptional customer experience. To be a known business that supplies its cakes rationalizable and have at least 3 branches outside of Gauteng.

Who We Bake For

  • Individuals buying gifts for birthday parties
  • Event planners - weddings, corporate business
  • Local customers looking for custom desserts
